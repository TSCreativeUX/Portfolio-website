THE EXPERT APP

Project Overview

The goal was to create an app that allows users to connect instantly with an Expert in virtually any field.

The challange was to creat an app which the expert indeed had experience in that field.

My role as a UX Designer included user research, usability testing and designing the application from scratch

User Personas

Wireframes

To gather a rough idea of how I envisioned EXPERT, I started wireframing with paper and pencil.

Later, I translated those low-fidelity wireframes into mid-fidelity, using Balsamiq.

Once I had a better understanding of EXPERT, I translated all my wireframes into high-fidelity wireframes using Sketch.

High-Fidelity Wireframes


Prototype and User Testing

By performing usability tests, I was able to gain a better understanding of how users interact with apps

because it highlights problem areas that might not have been obvious beforehand.

Polishing the Visual Design

Once I finished organzing the data collected from the usbaility tests, I continued to reviese my design of EXPERT.
